Evaluation of WEb Graphics

Website I Evaluated:

  1. Graphics fit in very well with the purpose and style of the page.
  2. The page loads very quickly, even with the many graphics featured on the page.
  3. Each graphic serves as an elaboration to what the article will be about.
  4. The featured graphics are not bright or obnoxious, they simply add on to the article's description.
  5. Some graphics have a few words included in the image to help summarize the preview.
  6. Every article link has a text description as well as a graphic.
  7. The graphics that do use text have a very good amout of contrast, making them very easy to read.
